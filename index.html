Rotation Rules
4 players: Across 6 matches, cycle through all 3 possible team combinations — 2 matches with each pairing.

5 players: One player sits out each match (loser's team rotates one out, sitting player joins the survivor). If a pair wins 2 matches in a row, they split before the 3rd — one stays, one joins the other side, and the surviving loser pairs with whoever's left.

6 players: Teams stay fixed for the entire day's session. Use 🎲 Suggest Today's Lineup at the start of each 6-player day.
